Level 3 Nachos

Nachos balls covered in spicy chips that comes together fast and flavors with a bit of spiciness in it. Mixed in ground beef, shreeded cheedar, diced green bell peepers, and taco seasoning, then rolled up into a ball and covered in your favorite type of spicy chips and deep fried into perfection. Served with a dip of your choice, wheather it's ranch dressing, hot sauce for more spiciness, or with a classic nacho cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of Ground Beef
  • 1 lb of Cheddar Cheese (Shredded)
  • Green Bell Peppers (Diced)
  • Salt and pepper, Chili Powder, Cumin, garlic powder, and Cayene pepper
  • Crunched Ships (Tortilla chips or any spicy chips)
  • Flour
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Shred the cheese Use your box grater and hold the cheese firmly while shreeding to avoid any injuries while do it.
  2. Combine Mix the ground beef with Salt and Pepper, Chili Powder, Cumin, Garlic Powder, and Cayene Pepper. Mix it well and add the Shreeded Cheese with your diced green bell peppers and mixed it again until they're fully combined.
  3. Crush the chips until they are perfectly fine and crumbly.
  4. Scoop up the mixture and roll them into palm-sized balls.
  5. Set up your breading station by whisking 2 eggs in a bowl, then add 1 cup of flower on the second bowl, and add in your chip mixture on the third bowl.
